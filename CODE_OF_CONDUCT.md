# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Bible Terminal is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
community for everyone, regardless of experience, identity, background, or
belief. Participation in this project is a privilege that depends on treating
other people with dignity.

## Expected behavior

Examples of behavior that supports the community include:

- communicating with empathy, patience, and respect;
- giving and accepting constructive technical feedback;
- focusing disagreement on ideas and observable behavior;
- acknowledging mistakes and making a good-faith effort to repair harm; and
- helping create an environment where people can participate safely.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or sustained disruption;
- insults, personal attacks, threats, or deliberately inflammatory comments;
- unwanted sexual attention or sexualized language or imagery;
- publishing another person's private information without permission;
- encouraging any of the behavior above; or
- conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
  community.

## Scope

This policy applies in the repository, issue and pull-request discussions, and
other project-managed spaces. It also applies when someone publicly represents
the project, such as by using an official project account or speaking on behalf
of the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Do not report conduct incidents in a public issue. Email
[vmrocha@gmail.com](mailto:vmrocha@gmail.com) with the subject
`[bible-terminal conduct]` and include relevant links, context, and any desired
resolution. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.

The maintainer will review reports fairly, avoid conflicts of interest, and
may request additional context. Responses will be proportionate to the conduct
and its impact and may include a private correction, warning, temporary
restriction, removal of content, or permanent exclusion from project spaces.
Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behavior is expected to comply immediately.

Retaliation against a person who makes a good-faith report or participates in
an investigation is not permitted.
